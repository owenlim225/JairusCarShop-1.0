# Welcome! Your First Steps Guide

**Are you completely new here?** This guide will walk you through everything step-by-step.

## What Is This Project?

Think of this as a **complete toolkit** for planning and building software projects. Instead of starting from scratch, you have:

- ✅ **Templates** - Pre-written documents you fill in
- ✅ **Guides** - Step-by-step instructions
- ✅ **AI Help** - Claude can assist with decisions
- ✅ **Structure** - Everything organized for you

**Real-world example:** Imagine you want to build a mobile app. This toolkit helps you:
1. Plan what the app does (planning sections)
2. Decide what technology to use (tech stack guide)
3. Organize your code (file structure)
4. Track progress (monitoring tools)

## Understanding the 12 Planning Sections

Think of these like chapters in a book. Each one covers a different part of planning:

### Week 1: Foundation (Sections 1-4)

**Section 1: Executive Summary**
- **What it is:** The "elevator pitch" of your project
- **Fill in:** What you're building, why it matters, who it's for
- **Time:** 30 minutes

**Section 2: Objectives & Success Metrics**
- **What it is:** How you'll measure success
- **Fill in:** Goals, KPIs, success criteria
- **Time:** 45 minutes

**Section 3: Scope Definition**
- **What it is:** What's included and what's not
- **Fill in:** Features, constraints, assumptions
- **Time:** 1 hour

**Section 4: System Architecture**
- **What it is:** Technical design of your system
- **Fill in:** Components, technology choices, diagrams
- **Time:** 2 hours

### Week 2: Planning Details (Sections 5-8)

**Section 5: Project Phases & Timeline**
- **What it is:** When things will happen
- **Fill in:** Phases, milestones, deadlines

**Section 6: Resource Planning**
- **What it is:** Who does what, budget
- **Fill in:** Team structure, costs

**Section 7: Risk Management**
- **What it is:** What could go wrong
- **Fill in:** Risks, mitigation plans

**Section 8: Execution Strategy**
- **What it is:** How you'll work day-to-day
- **Fill in:** Workflow, meetings, processes

### Week 3+: Operations (Sections 9-12)

**Section 9: Monitoring & Reporting**
- Track progress and metrics

**Section 10: ROI & Value Realization**
- Financial projections

**Section 11: Governance & Decision-Making**
- Who makes decisions

**Section 12: Lessons Learned**
- What you learned, improvements

## Common Questions

### "I don't know what to write in the templates"
**Answer:** That's okay! The templates have examples and placeholders. Start with what you know, even if it's rough. You can refine it later.

### "Which section should I start with?"
**Answer:** Always start with Section 1 (Executive Summary). It helps you clarify your vision, which makes the other sections easier.

### "What if I skip some sections?"
**Answer:** That's fine! Focus on what matters for your project. Sections 1-4 are most important for getting started.

### "How do I use Claude Skills?"
**Answer:** In Cursor (this editor), just ask Claude questions like:
- "What tech stack should I use for a web app?"
- "Help me create an architecture decision"
- "Review my code for quality"

### "Where do I actually write code?"
**Answer:** After planning, you'll write code in the `src/` directory. But first, complete your planning sections!
